Rira Institute of Technology

This University was founded on 2021, being the first University in the world to be built on the Blockchain.

RIT is an NFT collection based on the theme of engineering college and the world's first joint Japan-Korea PFP project. RIT was built by a woman named Rira, a missionary spreading the blockchain technology, with a support from MIX Foundation. She is a also friend of Satoshi, the developer of the blockchain technology. The students join the University with the slogan, "Develop the world and make it better through Web 3.0."

The university is using MIX as its base currency, and both teachers and students are studying to boost the future of MIX. There are 5 Departments in this University: Financial Engineering, Space Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Chemical Engineering, and Electronic Engineering. This very first University founded on the blockchain will definitely effect the world. If you learn as a student in this University, you may also make great changes.
